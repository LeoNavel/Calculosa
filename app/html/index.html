Functions

Use it when you want to do addition of two numbers

-

Shortcut: -
x

Use it when you want to multiply two numbers
Shortcuts: X or x or *
/
Division
Use it when you want to divide two numbers
Shortcut: /
ln
Natural logarithm
Use it when you want to get natural logarithm from number
Shortcut: L or l
^

Use it when you want power number Shortcut:^
